ProspectIQ – AI-Powered Human-in-the-Loop Business Development Assistant

ProspectIQ is an AI-powered business development prototype designed to automate the time-consuming process of prospect research and outreach preparation. Instead of manually analyzing companies and identifying potential clients, the system acts as an intelligent research assistant that understands a business, recommends qualified prospects, and prepares personalized communication for human review.

The workflow begins with a company website. ProspectIQ crawls and analyzes publicly available pages such as the homepage, About, Products, and Services sections to build a comprehensive understanding of the company's offerings, industry, value proposition, and target market. Using large language models, it extracts structured business insights, identifies the company's Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), and determines the types of organizations that would benefit most from its products or services.

Based on this analysis, the system evaluates a curated database of companies and ranks potential prospects according to industry relevance, business needs, growth stage, and alignment with the identified ICP. For every recommended prospect, ProspectIQ generates a detailed justification explaining why the company is a suitable lead, highlights potential pain points that the company's offerings could address, and assigns a confidence score to support decision-making.

Once qualified prospects are identified, the platform automatically drafts personalized outreach emails tailored to each company's business context. Rather than sending emails autonomously, ProspectIQ follows a human-in-the-loop workflow where every recommendation and outreach message is presented through a review dashboard. Users can inspect the generated insights, edit the content if necessary, approve or reject individual recommendations, and authorize email delivery only after manual validation.

By combining web scraping, AI-powered business understanding, intelligent lead qualification, personalized content generation, and human oversight, ProspectIQ significantly reduces the manual effort involved in business development while ensuring transparency, quality, and responsible AI-assisted decision-making.

Key Features
Website crawling and business intelligence extraction
AI-powered company and product analysis
Automatic Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) generation
Prospect qualification and lead scoring
Personalized outreach email generation
Human approval workflow before email delivery
Modular architecture for future CRM and automation integrations
Tech Stack
Frontend: React / Next.js
Backend: Node.js / Express
Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
Web Scraping: Playwright, Cheerio
AI: Ollama (Llama 3/Qwen) or other open-source LLMs
Email: Nodemailer (SMTP)
Deployment: Vercel / Render (planned)
